Intro to Programming

There are 5 ways of thinking:

Procedural Thinking --> Involves creating perfectly clear & unambiguous
instructions for a computer to follow.

Abstract Thinking --> Means finding similarity, generality amongst
seemingly different things.

System Thinking --> Break a big problem into smaller pieces.

Technological Thinking --> The ability to understand what a computer is & works.

Debugging --> Systematic process of relentlessly identifying the
cause of why a programm does not work.

HTML

HTML stands for Hyper Text Markup Language it is what you see on your
browser. This is how it work:

Text content --> What you see
Markup --> What it looks like
References to other Documents --> images + videos + etc.
Links to other pages --> Udacity

HTML Markup is made up of things called tags.
